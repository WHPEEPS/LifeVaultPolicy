Overview

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, date of birth
  • Executor contact details (name, email, phone, date of birth)
  • Asset information (financial, real estate, insurance, digital, valuables)
  • Voice will audio files
  • Recipient information (name, email, phone)

Purpose of Collection

  • Providing digital estate management services
  • Automatic notification to executor/recipients upon death detection

Storage Method & Retention

All data is encrypted with AES-256 and stored on Supabase servers (United States). Upon account deletion, all data is immediately and permanently erased.

Third-Party Disclosure

Collected information is not provided to third parties. The only exception is messages you specifically designate for delivery to your executor or named recipients, which are sent only to those individuals.

Your Rights

You may request account deletion at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, all your data is immediately and permanently removed.
